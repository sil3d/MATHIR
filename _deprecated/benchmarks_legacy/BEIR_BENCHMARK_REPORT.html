Key Findings

BM25 HURTS on SciFact

Equal-weight RRF hybrid drops -8.4pp nDCG@10 compared to dense-only. BM25's keyword matching introduces noise that degrades scientific claim verification quality.

CE Reranking HURTS When Using Hybrid Candidates

Cross-encoder reranking on hybrid (dense+BM25) candidates actuallydecreases performance. The BM25 candidates pollute the candidate set, confusing the reranker.

Dense-Only is the Right Approach

For scientific claim verification, pure dense retrieval (FAISS) dominates across all metrics. Semantic similarity outperforms keyword matching for technical content.
